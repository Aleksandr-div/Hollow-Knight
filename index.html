Resourses

Arcane Egg

Arcane Eggs are jet black stone eggs originating from a civilisation that existed before the Kingdom of Hallownest was founded

City Crest

Opens the gate between the Fungal Wastes and the City of Tears. The Mantis Claw is required to gain access to this area of the Fungal Wastes

Delicate Flower

The flowers originate from outside of Hallownest from a place known as "lands serene". They were brought to the kingdom by the great knight Ze'mer, who is now the Grey Mourner

Elegant Key

Opens the locked door that holds the Shade Soul Spell in Soul Sanctum, in the City of Tears. Inside is a Soul Warrior that continuously spawns Follies

Full Soul Vessel

3 Vessel Fragments complete a Soul Vessel and increases the Knight's maximum SOUL by 33

Geo

Used to buy goods from Merchants or to pay tolls for Stag Stations and Benches. Also used to pay the Nailsmith to upgrade the Knight's Nail

Hallownest Seal

Can be sold to Relic Seeker Lemm for 450 geo

King's Idol

Can be sold to Relic Seeker Lemm for 800 geo

Love Key

Opens the door inside the Tower of Love where the Collector can be fought

Lumafly Lantern

In dark areas, the Lumafly Lantern brightens up the area around the Knight, giving more visibility and allowing them to pay tolls to open Benches or doors

Mask Shard

Mask Shards are parts of Ancient Masks. 4 Mask Shards are needed to complete an Ancient Mask, which increases the Knight's health by one

Pale Ore

Pale Ore can be found in several places throughout Hallownest. Giving the Nailsmith Pale Ore along with a payment of Geo allows him to upgrade the Knight's Nail

Rancid Egg

A collectable item recovered from the corpses of Bluggsacs. Either an already-dead Bluggsac has to be found or a living one has to be killed to make it drop a Rancid Egg. Once a Bluggsac has been killed, it remains dead, even when resting at a Bench

Shopkeeper's Key

Presenting the key to Sly unlocks more of his wares

Simple Key

There are 4 locations that can be opened using Simple Keys

Tram Pass

Allows the Knight to ride the Tram. If the Tram is inspected before acquiring the pass, the description reads "A door with an open slot"

Wanderer's Journal

Can be sold to Relic Seeker Lemm for 200 geo

Bosses

Broken Vessel

Broken Vessel is one of the main bosses in Hollow Knight. It blocks the way to the Monarch Wings. Lost Kin is its dream variant

Brooding Mawlek

The Brooding Mawlek is a hidden boss in Hollow Knight as well as a Colosseum of Fools mini-boss

Brothers Oro & Mato

The Nailmaster Brothers Oro & Mato are a dual quest boss in Hollow Knight introduced in Godmaster. They sit at the top of the Pantheon of the Master in Godhome

Collector

The Collector is a hidden boss in Hollow Knight. Defeating it grants the Collector's Map

Crystal Guardian

The Crystal Guardian is an optional boss in Hollow Knight. The Enraged Guardian is its second, stronger and faster form. Both forms have to be beaten to unlock its Journal entry

Dung Defender

The Dung Defender is one of the main bosses in Hollow Knight. This champion of the past defends the access to Isma's Grove. The Hidden Dreams content White Defender is his dream variant

False Knight

False Knight is one of the main bosses in Hollow Knight. He keeps the City Crest required to open the gates to the City of Tears. Failed Champion is his faster and stronger dream form

Flukemarm

Flukemarm is a hidden boss in Hollow Knight. Defeating her grants a new Charm, Flukenest

God Tamer

God Tamer is a boss in Hollow Knight. She sits at the top of the Trial of the Fool

Great Nailsage Sly

Great Nailsage Sly is a quest boss in Hollow Knight introduced in Godmaster. He sits at the top of the Pantheon of the Sage in Godhome

Grimm

Troupe Master Grimm is both a quest NPC and boss introduced by the Grimm Troupe in Hollow Knight. He is the main character behind his quest. Nightmare King Grimm is his Dream form

Gruz Mother

Gruz Mother is an optional mini-boss in Hollow Knight. She is also a dual Colosseum of Fools boss. Unlocking her Journal entry requires beating 3 Gruz Mothers

Hive Knight

Hive Knight is an optional boss in Hollow Knight, introduced in Lifeblood. Defeating him grants a new Charm, Hiveblood

Hollow Knight

The Hollow Knight is one of the main bosses in Hollow Knight. it is central to the Knight's journey and the story of Hallownest. The Godmaster content Pure Vessel is Godseeker's idea of its stronger, "perfect" uninfected form

Hornet Protector

Hornet Protector is the first form of Hornet as a boss in Hollow Knight. She confronts the Knight in Greenpath to impede their quest, but underestimates her opponent and does not deploy her full strength. She must be defeated twice to unlock her Journal entry, but this can be either by defeating both forms or by defeating her Protector form once in Greenpath and once in Godmaster content Godhome

Mantis Lords

The Mantis Lords are an optional boss in Hollow Knight. Defeating them grants safe passage through the Mantis Village. The Godmaster content Sisters of Battle is their stronger form

Massive Moss Charger

The Massive Moss Charger is an optional mini-boss in Hollow Knight. It lies off the Pilgrim's Way in Greenpath

Nosk

Nosk is a hidden boss in Hollow Knight, Godmaster content Winged Nosk is its flying form. After defeating Nosk, a Pale Ore can be acquired

Oblobbles

The Oblobbles are a dual boss in Hollow Knight. They sit at the top of the Trial of the Conqueror. Two separate fights against them are required to unlock their Journal entry, either through replaying the trial or battling them in Godmaster content Godhome

Paintmaster Sheo

Paintmaster Sheo is a quest boss in Hollow Knight, introduced in Godmaster. He sits at the top of the Pantheon of the Artist in Godhome

Radiance

The Radiance is the true and secret final boss of Hollow Knight. Godmaster content Absolute Radiance is her perfect form. Despite only appearing in two of the five possible endings, she is the main antagonist of Hollow Knight

Soul Master

Soul Master is a main boss in Hollow Knight. Defeating him grants the Desolate Dive Spell. The Soul Tyrant is his dream variant

Soul Warrior

The Soul Warrior is a recurring mini-boss with varying difficulty in Hollow Knight. It appears twice in the Colosseum of Fools as a mini-boss. Unlocking its Journal entry requires defeating it twice

Traitor Lord

The Traitor Lord is a main boss in Hollow Knight. He blocks the path to the White Lady in the Queen's Gardens

Uumuu

Uumuu is a main boss in Hollow Knight. It guards Monomon the Teacher, one of the Dreamers

Vengefly King

The Vengefly King is an optional mini-boss in Hollow Knight as well as a Colosseum of Fools mini-boss. Unlocking its Journal entry requires defeating 2 Vengefly Kings

Watcher Knight

The Watcher Knights are a single main boss in Hollow Knight. They guard Lurien the Watcher, one of the three Dreamers. Their Journal entry is unlocked at the end of the fight, regardless of how many there were

Zote

Zote is a traveller from outside Hallownest who came to the Kingdom to fulfill a promise. This is ultimately revealed to be a promise of glory for himself
